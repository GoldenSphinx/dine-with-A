Domoda

(Peanut Stew)

A rich, creamy stew made with peanut butter, tomatoes, and vegetables, often served with rice.
It can include beef, chicken, or fish.

Benachin

(Jollof Rice)

A one-pot rice dish cooked with vegetables and either fish, chicken, or beef, seasoned with Gambian spices.

Key Ingredients: Rice, tomato paste, onions, carrots, eggplant, cabbage, protein of choice.

Superkanja

(Okra Stew)

A nutritious, thick stew made with okra, fish or meat, and palm oil, commonly eaten with rice.

Key Ingredients: Okra, fish or meat, palm oil, onions, and spices.

Chakery

(Sweet Couscous Pudding)

A creamy dessert made from steamed couscous mixed with sweetened yogurt, evaporated milk, and nutmeg.

Naan Mburu

(Gambian Millet Porridge)

A traditional porridge made from millet flour, milk, and sugar, often flavored with cinnamon or vanilla.

Key Ingredients: Millet flour, water, milk, sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg.

Ginger Cake

A soft, spicy cake infused with fresh ginger and sometimes served with honey or a light glaze.

Key Ingredients: Flour, fresh ginger, sugar, butter, eggs, and baking powder.
